Squelch Recipe

Requirements

    This will require a synthesizer with a coarse pitch control, and at least 2 bandpass filters that can operate in parallel.

Steps

    1. Start with a harmonically rich soundwave - generally a sawtooth wave because it contains every overtone, with a slow volume decay for each overtone.
    2. Create two bandpass filters in parallel - automating these around in space will create a formant sound
    3. Pitchbend the source wave form in conjunction with filter movement for the iconic squelch sound.
