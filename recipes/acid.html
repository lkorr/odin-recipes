Acid Recipe

Requirements

    This will require a subtractive synthesizer, with a lowpass filter with resonance and cutoff filter controls

Steps

    1. Start with a harmonically rich soundwave - generally a sawtooth wave because it contains every overtone, with a slow volume decay for each overtone.
    2. Apply a lowpass filter, with the cutoff assigned to the envelope.
    3. Increase resonance of the lowpass cutoff to taste, with some release to hear filter movement.
